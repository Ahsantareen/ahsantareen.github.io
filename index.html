About Me

I am a PhD researcher at Incheon National University (INU), South Korea, specializing in multi-energy microgrid operations, green hydrogen integration, and energy management systems. My research explores the integration of green hydrogen within multi-energy microgrids (MEMGs), addressing the interdependence of power and water systems. I focus on optimal sizing, operation, carbon emission reduction, EV scheduling, and energy trading mechanisms in complex energy networks.
